## Which Calabria comuni qualify for the 7% pension flat tax regime?

Foreign pensioners relocating to southern Italy may opt into Article 24-ter TUIR: a 7% substitute tax on all foreign-source income for up to nine tax years, with exemptions from IVIE, IVAFE, and Quadro RW reporting on qualifying overseas assets. Calabria is one of eight eligible regions (Sicily, Sardinia, Campania, Basilicata, Abruzzo, Molise, Puglia).

**MORE Group desk (Q2 2026):** non-resident closing averages 10% to 12% on second homes; model 21% cedolare secca and 5-year minimum hold before offer.

From 7 April 2026, Law 34/2026 raised the population ceiling from 20,000 to 30,000 residents measured by ISTAT at 1 January of the year preceding tax residency transfer. Calabria gained two newly eligible comuni in the 20,001-30,000 band: Castrovillari (Cosenza, about 20,605 residents) and Montalto Uffugo (Cosenza, about 20,034). Most Calabria tourism comuni already qualified under the prior threshold.

| Scalea | CS | 10,500 | Yes | Value coast STR |

| Rossano | CS | 12,000 | Yes | Inland value |

| Reggio Calabria | RC | 180,000+ | No | Urban rental only |

Eligibility requires foreign pension income, tax non-residency in Italy for five preceding years, transfer to a qualifying comune, and cooperation treaty with home country tax authority. The regime covers foreign-source income only; Italian rental income from Calabria property stays on cedolare secca or ordinary IRPEF. Property purchase is not mandatory but registered residence is.

Citability block: A US retiree with 45,000 euros annual Social Security considering Calabria property investment may transfer tax residency to Tropea (under 30,000 residents), elect Article 24-ter, and pay 7% substitute tax on foreign pension and portfolio income for nine years while purchasing a 160,000 euro two-bedroom as primary home at 2% registration tax under prima casa if residency is genuine. STR income from the same unit, if let while absent, is Italian-source and taxed separately at 21% or 26% cedolare secca. Reggio Calabria purchase does not unlock the pension regime at city level; Castrovillari or Scalea do.

Confirm comune population with commercialista using ISTAT data for your election year. This guide is not tax advice.

## What risks should Calabria property investors underwrite?

Calabria property investment rewards disciplined underwriting and punishes optimism copied from Puglia marketing decks without local conformita review. MORE Group Italy desk (Q2 2026) models 9% second-home registration tax, 21% cedolare secca on qualifying leases, and 5-year hold before compromesso deposit wires.

Seasonal STR concentration remains the primary cash-flow risk. July-August can deliver 50-60% of annual STR revenue on lungomare stock; winter weeks below 25% occupancy destroy pro formas that assume Amalfi pricing power.

Foreign resale liquidity trails Puglia and Sicily on Gate-away enquiry indices. Document CIN occupancy history and parking deeds to accelerate exit when marketing to Italian Milan-Rome family buyers who dominate summer demand.

Seismic classification on pre-1980 reinforced concrete stock requires engineer reports before corporate furnished tenants sign. Calabria sits in active seismic zones; retrofit costs can exceed one year of gross rent on 160,000 euro tickets if surveys were skipped at purchase.

Abusivismo and unpermitted terrace extensions appear on coastal listings. Full visura catastale, conformita edilizia, and geometric survey before compromesso are mandatory. Never waive independent avvocato review because ticket size is under 200,000 euros.
