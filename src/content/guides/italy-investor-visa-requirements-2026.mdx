## What are the core Italy investor visa requirements for 2026?

Non-EU nationals must commit to one of four Ministry-approved investment categories, prove clean criminal history, document the legal origin of funds through AML-grade bank trails, obtain Nulla Osta clearance, secure a consular visa valid for two years, register at the Questura within eight days of arrival, and transfer the full investment within three months. Property purchase does not count toward the investment threshold but remains the strongest accommodation proof for consular and police checks.

Legislative Decree 286/1998 Article 26-bis governs the program. The interministerial committee evaluates applications through the official Investor Visa portal managed by the Ministry of Enterprises and Made in Italy. Unlike elective residence, there is no passive-income test and no mandatory minimum stay for permit maintenance, though spending 183 days in Italy triggers tax residency unless you elect the Article 24-bis flat-tax regime described in our [Italy flat tax for new residents](/guides/italy-flat-tax-regime-new-residents/) guide.

## What investment tiers qualify under Italy investor visa requirements 2026?

Four capital thresholds define eligibility. You declare your category at Nulla Osta submission and cannot switch after approval without restarting the file. Each route requires the full amount to remain deployed for the initial two-year permit period.

| Investment tier | Minimum capital | Eligible target | Holding rule | Typical investor profile |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Innovative startups | €250,000 | Companies registered in the start-up innovative section | Equity held 2 years | Tech angels, venture-style risk tolerance |
| Active companies | €500,000 | Operating S.r.l. or S.p.A. with visura camerale | Shares or capital increase, 2 years | Operators injecting into Italian subsidiaries |
| Philanthropy | €1,000,000 | Culture, research, heritage, or immigration projects | Non-refundable donation | Legacy planners, no return expectation |
| Government bonds | €2,000,000 | BTP, CCT, or CTZ with 2+ years maturity | Custody at Italian financial institution | Sovereign-risk, capital-preservation focus |

Real estate, hospitality projects marketed as "golden visa apartments," and passive land banking are excluded. If a promoter ties residency to a property escrow, treat it as a compliance red flag and verify against the official tier table above.

## What documents does the Nulla Osta stage require?

The Nulla Osta is the digital pre-clearance that unlocks consular visa processing. Incomplete uploads restart the 30-day clock. Organize files in PDF, legible scans, and certified translations where noted.

| Document | Nulla Osta requirement | Format and validity | Common failure |
|---|---|---|---|
| Passport biodata | Full scan, all pages with stamps | Valid 3+ months beyond planned entry | Cropped scans missing signature page |
| Curriculum vitae | Education, career, source of wealth narrative | English or Italian, signed | Generic CV without wealth origin paragraph |
| Criminal record | Certificate from country of residence and any country lived 12+ months in last 5 years | Apostilled, issued within 90 days | Missing secondary-country certificate |
| Source of funds | Bank statements, tax returns, asset sale proofs | 24-month continuity preferred | Large unexplained inbound wires |
| Investment declaration | Signed commitment to chosen tier and timeline | Portal template, e-signature | Wrong tier amount or category typo |
| Health insurance | Schengen-wide coverage, zero deductible | €30,000 minimum per person | Travel policy excluding long-stay visa |
| Category evidence | Startup registry extract, company visura, bond custody letter, or donation project approval | Category-specific Ministry checklist | Shell company without active operations |

Upload through the official portal only. Third-party "visa agents" who cannot access your account should not hold originals. Keep a mirrored folder for consulate submission.

### Citability block: Nulla Osta in plain terms

The Nulla Osta is Italy's written no-objection for your investor visa file. You submit passport, criminal records, AML proof, insurance, and a signed pledge to invest €250,000 to €2,000,000 in an approved category. The interministerial committee targets 30 days for complete dossiers. Approval does not grant entry; it authorizes your local Italian consulate to issue the Type D visa. You then have six months to apply at consulate, enter Italy, register at Questura within eight days, and wire the full investment within three months. Missing the transfer window revokes the permit even if Nulla Osta was granted.

## What documents does the Italian consulate require after Nulla Osta?

Each consulate publishes a jurisdiction-specific list. Always download the current sheet for your legal residence region. Core items overlap Ministry review but consular officers re-verify originals in person or via courier protocols.

| Document | Consulate stage | Notes for 2026 practice |
|---|---|---|
| Nulla Osta PDF | Original approval letter with reference code | Must match portal status on interview day |
| Long-stay visa form | Type D, block letters, signed | One form per applicant including minors |
| Passport | Original plus two copies | Two blank pages minimum |
| Photos | ICAO standard, white background | Four copies typical for family kits |
| AML pack | Expanded source-of-funds file | Often stricter than Nulla Osta review |
| Accommodation proof | Registered lease, compromesso, or rogito | Address must match intended Questura comune |
| Insurance policy | Same coverage as Nulla Osta | Dates must cover visa validity |
| Consular fee | Non-refundable, payment method varies | Keep receipt in physical dossier |
| Translations | Certified Italian translations of foreign docs | Apostille chain intact |

Family dependents applying concurrently add marriage certificates, birth certificates, and dependency affidavits where parents over 65 join under reunification rules. Each dependent needs independent insurance unless the policy explicitly covers the household.

## What AML and source-of-funds proof do Italian authorities expect?

Anti-money laundering review is the top rejection driver after missed investment deadlines. Officers expect an unbroken narrative from earned or inherited wealth through bank accounts to the Italian investment account.

Acceptable evidence includes personal income tax returns for two to three years, employment contracts paired with payslips if wealth accumulated through salary, corporate audited accounts and dividend resolutions for business owners, notarized property sale deeds showing proceeds landing in your account, brokerage liquidation statements, inheritance probate orders, and trust distribution letters. Each foreign document needs apostille under the Hague Convention or consular legalization otherwise, plus sworn Italian translation.

Corporate structures require transparency: visura camerale, shareholder registers, and explanation of any intermediate holding companies. Transfers from high-risk jurisdictions trigger enhanced due diligence and can add weeks to Italian bank onboarding. Open the Italian custodian or operating account before Nulla Osta approval when possible so compliance checks finish before the three-month post-entry clock starts.

Wire transfers for the €500,000 company route must land in the target company's capital increase account with traceable SWIFT references matching your declaration. For €2,000,000 BTP purchases, the custody bank issues a holding certificate uploaded to the Ministry portal within the three-month window.

## What accommodation proof satisfies investor visa requirements?

Consulates and Questura officers reject tourist bookings, unregistered Airbnb stays, and addresses that fail local habitability standards. Acceptable proof includes a registered contratto di locazione of at least 12 months with Agenzia delle Entrate registration receipt, a registered compromesso showing deposit and completion timeline, or a rogito excerpt with cadastral identifiers.

The property must suit household size: a couple with two children needs sufficient bedrooms under municipal occupancy codes. Utilities must be activatable at handover. If you buy remotely, coordinate [buy property in Italy as a foreigner](/guides/buy-property-italy-foreigner/) rules with your visa timeline so compromesso dates align with consular interview slots.

Renting during construction is valid if the lease is registered and the unit is immediately habitable. Questura police checks later verify mailbox names and intercom labels match passport spellings.

## What is the three-month execution timeline after visa approval?

Treat the process as four gated phases. Slippage in Phase 4 revokes residency even when Phases 1 through 3 succeeded.

| Phase | Window | Mandatory actions | Documents to retain |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1: Nulla Osta | Days 1 to 30 | Portal upload, committee review | Approval PDF, submission receipt |
| 2: Consulate | Weeks 5 to 10 | Visa interview, fee payment | Visa stamp, fee receipt, returned originals |
| 3: Entry and Questura | Days 1 to 8 in Italy | Post office kit, fingerprints appointment | Ricevuta postale, Questura appointment slip |
| 4: Investment execution | 90 days from entry or permesso | Full wire, portal upload of proofs | Bank SWIFT, share certificates, bond custody statement |

Phase 3 detail: within eight days of crossing the border, purchase the revenue stamp (marca da bollo), complete the yellow permesso kit at an authorized Poste Italiane branch, and attend the Questura fingerprint session with passport, visa, photos, and accommodation proof. The post office ricevuta is your temporary legal status document until the plastic permesso arrives.

Phase 4 detail: transfer the entire tier amount, not a partial deposit. Upload Ministry portal proof before day 90. Startup route requires registry confirmation of equity; bond route requires custodian certificate; donation route requires recipient foundation acknowledgment.

## How do family dependents fit Italy investor visa requirements 2026?

The main investor's capital satisfies the entire household. Spouse, minor children, adult children with documented disability, and dependent parents over 65 under Article 29 reunification rules may obtain linked permessi without additional investment.

Each dependent submits passport, criminal record where age requires it, marriage or birth certificates (apostilled and translated), health insurance, and proof of dependency. Children over 18 who are financially independent cannot piggyback on the investor file. Parents must show no capable children abroad or documented medical need.

Family permits mirror the investor's two-year initial validity and renew on the same three-year cycles while the underlying investment remains compliant. Dependents may work and study in Italy without separate labor market tests.

## What are the most common rejection reasons in 2026?

Understanding failure patterns prevents costly restarts. These issues appear repeatedly in consular and Ministry feedback.

**Weak or broken AML trails.** Unexplained six-figure deposits, crypto cash-outs without exchange statements, or funds arriving from unrelated third parties trigger automatic holds. Fix by documenting each hop with dated bank PDFs.

**Missing the three-month investment deadline.** Italian bank AML onboarding averages four to six weeks for first-time non-resident accounts. Start banking relationships during Nulla Osta review, not after landing.

**Ineligible €500,000 company targets.** Real estate holding shells, dormant S.r.l. entities, or companies without active revenue fail Ministry audit. Demand visura camerale showing ongoing operations before signing share purchase agreements.

**Accommodation defects.** Unregistered leases, properties under major renovation without habitability certificate, or mismatched addresses between visa forms and rental contracts cause consulate refusal.

**Category misrepresentation.** Marketing materials promising "property golden visa" conflate separate legal tracks. Officers reject files where declared startup investment is actually a developer escrow.

**Insurance gaps.** Policies with deductibles, geographic exclusions, or durations ending before visa validity are common technical denials easily avoided with residency-grade insurers.

## How should you prepare a master checklist before applying?

Use this consolidated pre-flight list spanning all three administrative layers.

**Identity and background:** valid passport, apostilled criminal records from every relevant jurisdiction, CV with wealth narrative, four passport photos per person.

**Financial and AML:** three years tax returns, 24 months bank statements, asset sale or inheritance proofs, Italian bank account or custodian opened, investment account ready for tier transfer.

**Program commitment:** Nulla Osta portal account, signed tier declaration, category-specific target documentation, health insurance certificates.

**Accommodation:** registered lease or property contract with cadastral data, utility readiness confirmation, mailbox name order placed.

**Consulate and arrival:** jurisdiction checklist printed, fee payment method confirmed, post office kit location mapped near intended comune, Questura appointment research completed.

**Post-entry compliance:** calendar alerts at day 7 and day 75 for Questura and investment deadlines, legal counsel retained for portal uploads, copies of every receipt stored offline.
