## About Near and the UpTown Masterplan

[Near Milan](/developers/near-milan/) partners with municipal EuroMilano to deliver Cascina Merlata's residential cluster. The UpTown brand groups multiple towers with shared district infrastructure: landscaped public realm, ground-floor retail pods, and M1 metro connectivity.

Bliss UpTown occupies the on-market segment of that platform while Inspire captures early off-plan pricing and Feel targets delivering-phase buyers needing faster income or occupancy.

Investors evaluating Bliss should model total cost against Inspire's €348,500 entry and Feel's €517,000 delivering threshold. On-market Bliss units may price between those bands depending on floor, exposure, and finish tier.

## Investment Pros and Cons

### Advantages

- On-market status reduces off-plan completion uncertainty versus Inspire 2028
- UpTown masterplan scale supports district maturation and retail fill-out
- MIND-linked employment underpins qualified tenant pipelines
- Near/EuroMilano institutional backing versus small promoter risk
- Metro M1 preserves locational value for commuter tenants

### Risks

- Bliss pricing may exceed Inspire pre-sales without compensating handover advantage
- Multiple UpTown towers may temporarily increase rental supply
- Emerging district still building social and retail infrastructure
- STR licensing not automatic in new condominiums
- Agency-listed stock requires sharper due diligence on seller vs developer principal

## Rental Strategy

Long-term furnished leases to MIND and metro-linked corporate tenants remain the default compliant strategy. Model net yields after 25% management if using operators, IMU, and condominium extraordinary works reserves.

Short-term rental can lift summer gross revenue but adds platform fees, cleaning, and cedolare secca at 21% or 26% on gross rent. Verify building regolamento before underwriting STR.

## Buyer Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Buyer who missed Inspire pre-sales

Bliss or Feel on-market may be optimal if Inspire wait until 2028 is unacceptable. Compare €/sqm premium against 24 months of lost rent.

### Scenario 2: Corporate tenant targeting MIND

Prioritize units with parking and efficient layouts for two-bedroom corporate leases. Bliss on-market may allow faster furnishing than off-plan.

### Scenario 3: Portfolio investor holding Inspire already

Bliss adds on-market exposure only if diversification benefits outweigh correlated district risk across multiple UpTown towers. Model vacancy jointly with Inspire handover timing so two towers do not compete for the same MIND tenant pool in the same quarter.

## Closing verification checklist

Before compromesso on Bliss UpTown on-market stock, verify seller identity, agency mandate, and condominium health in writing.

**Rogito readiness.** Confirm habitability certificate, snagging completion, and warranty transfer documents on near-complete units. On-market Bliss inventory may be developer principal or resale; title chain differs materially between those paths.

**Parking and storage.** Trace deeded parking slot or verified lease in the notary bundle. UpTown towers often sell parking separately; missing deeds block corporate tenant leases from MIND employers.

**Condominium review.** Obtain certificato di regolarità contributi and read regolamento on affitti brevi. Northwest Milan new builds may restrict STR even when long-term furnished leases remain permitted.

**Tax and rental setup.** Register intended rental regime before marketing. CIN application for STR requires comune compliance. Model IMU on Cascina Merlata cadastral categories and registration tax at 2% prima casa or 9% second home.

**Agency mandate check.** If Bliss is agency-listed, confirm commission structure does not inflate price above Inspire pre-sales without compensating handover advantage. Request comparable closed transactions in the same tower within six months.

Wire purchase funds only to the notaio conto provvisorio after your avvocato clears the compromesso.
