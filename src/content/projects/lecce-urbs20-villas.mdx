## About Urbs20 and Grimaldi Store Lecce

Urbs20 positions semi-detached villas within Lecce city limits rather than Salento countryside contrade. The site sits adjacent to Parco del Galateo, a recently completed green zone, between Via Romagna, Via Bruno Cantobelli, and Via Monsignor Antonio Giancane in a residential belt that balances centro walkability with quieter tree-lined streets.

Grimaldi Store Lecce markets the construction phase with emphasis on Class A3 envelopes, controlled mechanical ventilation, and rooftop or garden outdoor living on upper and ground-floor typologies respectively.

Ground-floor units receive front and rear private gardens for indoor-outdoor living. First-floor units include large terraces plus exclusive roof garden levels with integrated mini-pool options on marketing materials for upper-tier layouts.

## Closing verification checklist

Before compromesso on Urbs20 off-plan stock, verify milestone schedule, garage title, and energy system scope in writing.

**Price inclusion audit.** Confirm photovoltaic, solar thermal, VMC, garage box, and outdoor mini-pool or roof garden features match the marketed listing tier before deposit.

**Construction milestones.** Tie payments to certified stages: foundation, envelope, MEP, finishes, habitability. Delay penalties should reference realistic urban Lecce build timelines.

**Garage access.** Private stair or lift connection to basement box must be deeded in notary bundle, not merely described in marketing brochures.

**STR planning.** If underwriting rental income, verify comune CIN path for new-build villas in this residential zone before marketing.

Wire funds only after avvocato clears compromesso. See [due diligence Italy property](/guides/due-diligence-italy-property/) and [buy property Italy foreigner](/guides/buy-property-italy-foreigner/).

**Off-plan milestone audit.** Request written schedule tying each payment to foundation, envelope, MEP, and habitability certificates. Delay penalties must reference realistic Lecce urban build timelines, not generic developer templates copied from northern Italy towers.

**Roof garden and pool scope.** Marketing renders for upper units may show mini-pools and roof gardens as optional upgrades. Confirm which features are included in your quoted €425,000 or €485,000 tier before reservation deposit.

**Garage private access.** Private lift or stair to basement box must appear in preliminary notary bundle, not only in showroom brochures.
