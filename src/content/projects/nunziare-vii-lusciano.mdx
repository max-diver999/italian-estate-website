## About Nunziare VII

Nunziare VII extends Cecere Management's Nunziare Luxury Projects line with a compact eight-unit building on Via Gambardella where Lusciano meets Aversa Sud. The scheme faces the planned Parco della Legalità, a 50,000 sqm public green space that anchors urban regeneration narratives for buyers comparing Campania new-build quality against coastal STR stock in Scalea or Palermo value corridors.

Construction uses Ytong aerated concrete for thermal and acoustic performance combined with full-electric plant design: no gas connection, heat pumps for heating, cooling, and domestic hot water, and envelope detailing aimed at Nearly Zero Energy Building standards. Developer marketing cites up to 90% energy savings versus traditional masonry construction on comparable floor areas.

Unit mix includes two ground-floor apartments with private gardens and car access, intermediate floors with livable terraces, and a top-floor attic with approximately 140 sqm private solarium predisposto for pool and pergola bioclimatica installations on select listings marketed through House24 and TrovaCasa portals in 2026.

## Off-Plan Mechanics and Customization

Cecere Management targets construction start in March-April 2026 with delivery at the beginning of 2028 on published schedules. Buyers typically pay reservation deposit, sign compromesso with 10-20% due, then fund milestone payments tied to structural and systems completion before rogito balance at handover.

Interior finish packages remain customizable pre-construction on many units, allowing buyers to select premium flooring, kitchen lines, and bathroom specifications within developer-approved supplier lists. Independent avvocato should verify milestone penalty clauses and bank-guarantee structures on deposits before wire transfers to developer escrow accounts.

Off-plan buyers should model 18-24 month capital lock-up without STR income and budget 9-11% transaction costs on €400,000 tickets including registration tax at primary-home or second-home rates chosen with commercialista before compromesso signing.
