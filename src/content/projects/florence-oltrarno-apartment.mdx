## Heritage Renovation and STR Compliance

Oltrarno palazzi often combine centuries-old timber beams with post-2000 electrical upgrades that fail to meet current STR insurance requirements until geometra certifies load and fire egress paths. Renovation budgets on partial stock run €80,000-150,000 beyond purchase price when kitchens, bathrooms, and MEP must meet modern tenant expectations behind UNESCO facades.

Soprintendenza review timelines on visible window replacements extend 3-6 months and can delay rogito if sellers marketed turnkey status without approved filings. Buyers should commission pre-compromesso geometra audit listing every non-conforming element with costed remediation schedule.

STR operators near Santo Spirito bars face acoustic neighbor complaints more frequently than north-bank hotel district stock because residential families occupy upper floors above ground-level nightlife. Soundproofing interior windows and enforcing quiet hours in guest contracts reduce delicensing risk.

Student lease investors should target September lease start with August furniture installation buffer because Florence SMN arrival days create peak demand for short-term storage and cleaning vendors. Lease contracts specifying no-subletting and quarterly inspections protect against unauthorized STR conversion by tenants.

Transaction stack on €500,000 Oltrarno second-home purchase runs 10-12% including 9% registration tax, notary, and agency fees. Furnished STR launch adds €10,000-20,000 for compliant furniture, CIN registration, and professional photography before first booking.

Resale liquidity improves on elevator-served palazzi with legal terraces because US and UK buyers reject walk-up units above second floor without lift despite charming interior finishes. Listing periods of 4-8 months are typical on correctly priced legal terrace stock.
