## Agriturismo vs STR Decision Framework

Buyers planning agriturismo must document vineyard rows, olive harvest, or crop production meeting commune minimum hectares before hospitality SCIA conversion. Commercialista setup for agriturismo VAT and staffing rules adds administrative overhead versus simple furnished STR with cedolare secca at 21%.

Greve in Chianti STR without agriturismo license still performs April-October when marketed to US wine tourists and Florence day-trip guests. Radda upsell positioning commands higher nightly rates with smaller unit inventory and stricter parking requirements on hill lanes.

Restoration contractors in Chianti Classico often book 12-18 month lead times during peak renovation seasons. Early compromesso with phased contractor scheduling prevents missing next summer rental launch windows after habitability certificate issuance.

Energy retrofit grants occasionally apply to rural Tuscany buildings when combined with agricultural business registration. Verify current regional incentive programs with commercialista before modeling restoration capex offsets.

Castellina and Gaiole contrada often trade lower entry tickets than Greve with thinner restaurant tourism footfall but stronger privacy for owners wanting vineyard silence. Buyers comparing commune sub-markets should visit each centro on weekend evenings before selecting restoration target contrada.

Chianti Classico wine event calendar drives April and October occupancy spikes independent of summer beach tourism. Marketing casali around vendemmia harvest experiences can lift shoulder rates when paired with licensed wine tour partnerships.

IMU on agricultural casali varies with land hectares and primary residence classification. Commercialista review before rogito clarifies whether owner use weeks trigger different IMU brackets than pure investment holds.

Stone roof and timber beam inspections should precede any interior design planning because structural scope changes after compromesso often void contractor quotes fixed on optimistic seller disclosures.

Greve weekend traffic on SS222 wine route affects guest arrival times during harvest festivals. Properties with clear lane access and private parking outperform walk-only casali on guest review logistics scores.
