## Centro STR and License Reality

Florence municipal policy halted new tourist rental registrations inside UNESCO centro to protect resident quality of life after post-2015 STR density growth. Buyers underwriting STR yields on centro listings must verify transferable existing CIN and commune registration on exact address rather than assuming neighbor building compliance extends to target unit.

Long-term furnished leases to American university programs, EU agency staff, and remote workers deliver 2-3.5% gross on €600,000-900,000 tickets with September-June academic calendars reducing summer-only dependency. Corporate tenants prefer elevator-served palazzi with legal terraces and fast broadband documented in listing materials.

Licensed existing STR on transferable permits may still achieve 3-5% gross when professional managers market Duomo-walkable addresses to international art tourists. Permit transfer requires avvocato review of seller declarations and commune confirmation before deposit because delicensed addresses cannot reopen STR under current rules.

## Investment Analysis

Gross yields on €500,000-800,000 restored centro units often land in 2-4% range depending on STR permit status and elevator presence. Net yields compress after IMU, management at 20-25%, and rental tax regimes chosen with commercialista guidance on furnished stays.

Capital appreciation tracked strong foreign enquiry 2015-2022 in Duomo-adjacent quarters with moderation after STR enforcement headlines. UNESCO scarcity supports long-term character premiums despite yield compression versus southern Italy value markets on similar deployed capital.

Palazzo walk-up floors without elevator discount 10-20% versus comparable lifted units but may still lease to young tourism demographics accepting short centro stays when STR permits allow. Terrace legality materially affects pricing because illegal enclosures discovered during geometra surveys trigger remediation costs and resale discounts.

Transaction stack on €600,000 centro second-home purchase runs 10-12% including 9% registration tax, notary, and agency commission when applicable. Furnished lease launch adds €12,000-25,000 for compliant furniture and broadband installation before first tenant occupancy.

Resale liquidity improves on elevator-served palazzi with documented conformità because US and UK buyers reject third-floor walk-ups without lift despite charming interior finishes. Listing periods of 6-12 months are typical on correctly priced legal terrace stock versus years on mispriced units with hidden heritage defects.

Tranio listing refresh cycles require re-verification at offer stage because centro apartments sell quickly to domestic buyers during spring academic lease season when foreign enquiry spikes on portal marketing.

## Heritage Renovation Depth

Centro palazzi combine centuries-old timber beams with post-2000 electrical upgrades that fail STR insurance requirements until geometra certifies load and fire egress paths. Partial renovation stock runs €80,000-180,000 beyond purchase when kitchens, bathrooms, and MEP must meet modern tenant expectations behind UNESCO facades.

Soprintendenza review timelines on visible window replacements extend 3-6 months and can delay rogito if sellers marketed turnkey status without approved filings. Buyers should commission pre-compromesso geometra audit listing every non-conforming element with costed remediation schedule before price acceptance.

Soundproofing interior windows reduces acoustic neighbor complaints in dense palazzi where residential families occupy upper floors above ground-level retail. STR operators on transferable permits should enforce quiet hours in guest contracts to protect license renewal.

Student and fellowship lease investors target September start with August furniture buffer because Santa Maria Novella arrival days create peak demand for storage and cleaning vendors. Lease contracts specifying no-subletting protect against unauthorized STR conversion by tenants in license-sensitive buildings.

Pair centro prestige hold with [Chianti farmhouse restoration](/projects/chianti-farmhouse-restoration/) when buyers want split Tuscany portfolio balancing agriturismo yield against UNESCO urban address.
