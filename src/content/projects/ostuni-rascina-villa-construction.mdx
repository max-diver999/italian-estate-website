International buyers encounter the group through English-language portals and agency-led viewings coordinated with local geometric and notary partners.

Contrada Rascina sits in the olive-country belt south and east of Ostuni's white city, where foreign demand concentrates on villas with pools, sea glimpses, and manageable drives to Adriatic beaches. Plots above 10,000 sqm are increasingly scarce as agricultural land conversion rules tighten.

## Rental and Yield Outlook

Ostuni countryside villas with pools often achieve €120-180 nightly July-August with shoulder season €80-110. Annual gross yields of 5-7% are achievable with 55-70% weighted occupancy on well-managed STR, higher than Milan but with seasonal voids.

Long-term annual leases to expat families run €1,200-1,800 monthly for quality three-bedroom villas, producing 4-5% gross on €400,000-600,000 all-in cost bases. Net yields depend on management (20-25% STR), utilities, and IMU.

## Purchase Process for Under-Construction Rural Villas

1. Geometric and cadastral survey independent of seller
2. Building permit, SCIA, and abusi clearance review
3. Compromesso with milestone payments tied to construction stages
4. Completion guarantee or escrow if developer-led
5. Rogito at habitability certificate with final pool compliance check

Budget 10-12% transaction costs plus €5,000-12,000 for survey, legal, and pool compliance verification. See [due diligence Italy property](/guides/due-diligence-italy-property/) and [Puglia property investment guide](/guides/puglia-property-investment-guide/).
