## About Ravello Villa Stock

Ravello sits on a limestone headland above the Amalfi Coast with garden villas, music-festival cultural branding, and views that JamesEdition and luxury agency portfolios market to American and British HNWI seeking Mediterranean trophy assets below French Riviera absolute peaks on comparable garden scale.

Villa inventory ranges from restored three-bedroom terraces with mature lemon groves to raw casali requiring €300,000-600,000 MEP and pool remediation behind heritage-compliant facades. Buyers should commission relazione geologica and PAI landslide zoning review before compromesso on any cliff-adjacent terrace marketed with infinity pool photography.

Ravello STR calendar peaks around summer music events and July-August Gulf tourism with shoulder seasons supporting wedding and corporate retreat packages when villas include staffed kitchens and documented parking for guest transfers on SS163 cliff roads.

## UNESCO Vincolo and Geotechnical Risk

Costiera Amalfitana UNESCO status and vincolo paesaggistico restrict exterior terrace extensions, pool excavation, and facade alterations on visible hillside plots. Soprintendenza review timelines extend 6-12 months on terrace projects marketed as turnkey in agency photography without filed SCIA documentation.

Steep terrain and winter rainfall make geotechnical due diligence mandatory on Ravello contrada stock. Request relazione geologica, inspect retaining walls and drainage on terrace villas, and verify SS163 access rights before underwriting renovation budgets on cliff-edge plots with historical frana exposure in municipal PAI maps.

Licensed STR requires national CIN plus Campania CIR registration with Ravello comune display rules. Professional operators with three or more listings face additional SCIA obligations from 2026 reviewed with commercialista before scaling portfolios.

## Investment Analysis

Gross yields on €1,800,000-3,500,000 restored garden villas often land in 2.5-4% range when licensed for ultra-luxury summer STR with €2,000-5,000 nightly peaks on event weeks. Net yields compress after IMU at trophy cadastral bands, management at 25-35% on staffed operations, and STR tax regimes chosen with commercialista guidance.

July and August frequently generate 45-55% of annual STR revenue on managed Ravello portfolios per Amalfi Coast seasonality benchmarks in our [Amalfi Coast investment guide](/guides/amalfi-coast-property-investment-guide/). Model 120-140 rentable nights annually rather than calendar-year occupancy claims in agency pro formas.

Capital preservation thesis dominates: correctly priced garden villas with geotechnical clearance and terrace conformità may wait 18-36 months for qualified HNWI buyer versus months on Sorrento apartments at lower per sqm with broader Anglo-American resale audience.

Renovation of raw casali runs €250,000-600,000 for MEP, kitchens, pool structural engineering, and terrace remediation meeting UNESCO and insurance standards before ultra-luxury marketing photography begins.

Transaction stack on €2,000,000 second-home purchase runs 10-15% including registration tax, notary, geotechnical survey, and heritage consultancy fees. Ultra-luxury STR launch adds €40,000-80,000 for designer furniture and concierge onboarding before first guest season.

Pair Ravello trophy exposure with [Sorrento coastal apartment](/projects/sorrento-coastal-apartment/) gateway stock when buyers want split Amalfi gateway cash-flow and headland garden trophy layering on diversified Campania calendars.

SS163 summer congestion can add 45-60 minutes to Naples airport transfers during peak August weeks. Villa owners marketing STR should document helicopter and private transfer partners for ultra-HNWI guest expectations on cliff-road logistics.

## Capital Stack and Carry Notes

Non-resident buyers rarely secure Italian mortgage leverage on €1.5M+ cliff villas because banks underwrite geotechnical risk slowly when rental income is ultra-seasonal STR. Cash or cross-border mortgage buyers dominate Ravello transactions, which speeds rogito to 90-120 days when geotechnical and conformità packets are complete pre-compromesso.

Annual carry on €2,000,000 villa often includes €15,000-30,000 IMU at trophy cadastral bands, €8,000-20,000 garden and pool maintenance on terraced plots, and €12,000-25,000 management retainers when owners want staffed check-in during festival weeks.
