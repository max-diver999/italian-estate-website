## Foreign Buyer Acquisition Path

EU citizens open the standard path: codice fiscale, compromesso with deposit cap review, notary rogito, and IMU registration. Non-EU reciprocity-country buyers follow identical rogito mechanics with additional embassy documentation in some jurisdictions. Corporate purchase via Italian SPV remains rare at mid-market tickets but suits portfolio investors consolidating multiple Puglia lots.

Off-plan buyers should structure milestone payments tied to foundation, structural closure, MEP completion, and habitability certificate rather than large upfront deposits without bank guarantees. Italian law permits fideiussione bank bonds on promoter contracts when negotiated before compromesso.

STR operators need SCIA tourist rental authorization, cedolare secca registration at 21% on furnished short stays, and commune occupancy reporting where required. Professional managers in Ostuni corridor typically charge 20-25% of gross booking revenue including cleaning and guest communication.

Currency exposure matters for UK and US buyers funding euro purchases from foreign accounts. Locking exchange rates at compromesso versus rogito can swing total cost several percent on €400,000 tickets.

Post-rogito utilities, internet activation, and pool maintenance contracts should be budgeted before first guest arrival. Class A new-build envelopes reduce electricity surprises versus poorly insulated restored masseria marketing the same aesthetic label.
