## About Apulia Deluxe Masseria Product

Masseria estates represent the upper tier of Apulia Deluxe portfolio below combined trulli-villa flagship compounds but above entry restoration projects. Traditional masseria forms use thick stone walls, vaulted interiors, and courtyard layouts adapted for modern luxury pools and outdoor dining terraces.

Apulia Deluxe coordinates landscape permits, pool SCIA, and building approvals that foreign buyers struggle to navigate on agricultural contrada land. Olive grove settings provide photographic authenticity for luxury STR listings targeting UK, US, and Northern European guests seeking experiential Puglia stays.

Ostuni's white-city brand and Valle d'Itria proximity support premium nightly rates for estates that combine pool compounds with authentic stone architecture. Professional management partners in the Ostuni corridor report strong demand for masseria stock with four-plus bedrooms and licensed tourist rental SCIA.

Pricing remains POA because land hectares, restoration depth, pool specifications, and guest annex volumes vary materially across lots. Buyers should budget €800,000 at standard luxury finish rising toward €1,500,000 for ultra-premium customization with wine cellars, staff quarters, and commercial kitchens.

## Investment Analysis

Luxury masseria STR in Ostuni corridor commands premium nightly rates when licensed, professionally photographed, and managed with concierge services. Gross revenue models often assume €200-350 nightly peak with 65-75% summer occupancy on four-bedroom estates with pools.

Net yields after IMU at roughly 0.76-1.06% on primary homes or higher on second homes, management at 20-25%, utilities, pool maintenance, and cedolare secca at 21% on furnished short stays typically land in the 3-5% net range on €800,000-1,000,000 tickets.

Capital appreciation in Ostuni premium countryside has tracked strong foreign demand since 2020, though liquidity remains thinner than Milan or Rome urban stock. Heritage and landscape protection limits oversupply, supporting long-term character premiums.

Long-term furnished leases deliver lower gross yields around 3-4% with simpler compliance but miss masseria peak summer rate spikes.

Apulia Deluxe buyers should request reference estates with completed pool handovers and STR track records before POA negotiations. Developer permit packages accelerate entry but do not replace independent counsel on agricultural land categories and UNESCO landscape pool placement rules for each contrada lot.
