## About Portofino Apartment Stock

Portofino village offers perhaps Europe's tightest luxury residential envelope: no meaningful new-build infill, extreme landscape protection, and global brand recognition that supports trophy pricing disconnected from yield math. Engel & Voelkers Liguria markets harbour-adjacent apartments and headland units requiring interior modernization while preserving park-compliant exteriors.

Inventory mixes fully restored turnkey apartments with raw units needing €200,000-500,000 renovation for MEP, bathrooms, and terrace waterproofing behind historic facades. Buyers should commission geometra and park authority pre-checks before compromesso because illegal terrace enclosures destroy resale value when discovered during buyer surveys.

US and British yachting families dominate enquiry alongside Swiss wealth holders cross-shopping French Riviera addresses and [Bellagio villa lake view](/projects/bellagio-villa-lake-view/) trophy stock on Lake Como.

## Parco di Portofino Compliance

Most Portofino peninsula sits inside regional natural park with strict height, façade colour, and landscape controls. Exterior renovations require park and municipal approval timelines extending 6-12 months on visible terrace and window projects. Interior SCIA/CILA paths proceed faster when geometra confirms no structural impact on protected facades.

Buyers underwriting renovation budgets should add 25-40% contingency versus standard Ligurian refurbishment because material palettes and park inspector visits add cost and delay. Illegal historic terrace extensions remain common due diligence failures in agency photography.

Licensed STR faces commune density scrutiny and condominium rules on sub-30-day stays in dense village buildings where permanent residents occupy upper floors above harbour restaurants.

## Investment Analysis

Gross yields on €1,200,000-2,500,000 renovated apartments often land in 2-3% range when licensed for ultra-luxury summer STR with €1,200-2,500 nightly peaks on harbour terraces. Winter voids compress annual percentages unless owners pivot to long-term lease to Milan finance executives accepting seasonal village quiet.

Net yields compress after IMU at trophy cadastral bands, management at 25-35% on staffed operations, and cedolare secca on furnished stays. Capital preservation thesis dominates: correctly priced harbour stock may wait 18-36 months for qualified HNWI buyer versus months in Santa Margherita at lower per sqm.

Renovation of raw apartments runs €250,000-600,000 for MEP, kitchens, and terrace remediation meeting park and insurance standards before marketing photography begins.

Transaction stack on €1,500,000 second-home purchase runs 10-12% including registration tax, notary, and heritage consultancy fees. Ultra-luxury STR launch adds €30,000-60,000 for designer furniture and concierge onboarding before first guest season.

Compare marina lifestyle step-down against [Monte Argentario sea view](/projects/monte-argentario-sea-view/) when buyers want Tyrrhenian yachting exposure at lower absolute tickets with Tuscan resale narratives.

## Trophy Liquidity and Carry Costs

Portofino resale liquidity depends on harbour view permanence and parking deed clarity more than interior designer finishes because HNWI buyers commission bespoke renovations after acquisition anyway. Mispriced stock with illegal terrace enclosures can linger 24-36 months while compliant harbour units attract off-market enquiries within one yachting season when E&V or local brokers circulate teasers to Swiss family databases.

Annual carry on €1,500,000 apartment often includes €12,000-25,000 IMU at trophy cadastral bands, €6,000-15,000 condominium and building maintenance on historic palazzi, and €8,000-20,000 management retainers when owners want staffed check-in during regatta weeks. Owners who self-manage between personal visits should still budget concierge partnerships for luggage and restaurant reservations because guest satisfaction scores drive repeat ultra-luxury bookings.

Currency hedging matters for UK and US buyers when offers anchor to sterling or dollar purchasing power rather than Italian comparables alone during euro weakness cycles.

Independent insurance review on palazzo stairwells and shared roof structures should precede ultra-luxury STR launch because guest liability exposure rises when harbour restaurants operate beneath residential floors in dense village lanes.
