## About Okam Italy and the Maciachini Site

[Okam Italy](/developers/okam-italy/) expanded in 2026 with the Maciachini acquisition, continuing a strategy of converting underutilised urban logistics and office stock into residential product where Milan planning allows functional change. Industry reporting notes OKAM Italy's CEO Ariel Bruckmayer emphasising green space valorisation and dwelling quality on consolidated urban parcels.

The Maciachini asset previously housed Tillmanns Spa operational and headquarters functions. Functional conversion to residential requires planning approval, design coordination with a primary architecture studio, and construction phasing typical of Lombardy regeneration timelines.

Foreign buyers already familiar with Okam through Navigli projects should expect similar sales mechanics: reservation deposit, compromesso, milestone payments, and rogito at habitability. Independent legal review remains mandatory.

## Closing verification checklist

Before reservation on Maciachini Urban Retreat pre-launch stock, verify Okam Italy corporate standing, planning trajectory, and milestone escrow mechanics in writing.

**Pre-sales readiness.** Okam lists timetable and pricing as coming soon. Do not wire large deposits until compromesso specifies unit mix, net areas, delay penalties, and Q-target handover dates tied to certified construction progress.

**Planning and conversion.** Maciachini converts former Tillmanns industrial headquarters. Request urban planning approval references, functional change documentation, and architect studio contract identity before matching Navigli Okam deposit levels.

**Payment structure.** Mirror Prandina and Meli milestone schedules: reservation, compromesso 10-20%, foundation, structure, systems, rogito balance. Confirm bank escrow or completion guarantees on each tranche.

**Condominium draft rules.** Review early regolamento drafts on affitti brevi and parking allocation. North-west Milan tenants often require metro walkability and quiet interiors; verify noise studies if site abuts residual logistics uses.

**Tax election.** Model registration tax at 2% prima casa or 9% second home before reservation. Non-resident buyers budget 10-12% total transaction costs on Lombardy second homes including notary and geometric survey.
