## Chiavi in Mano Model for Foreign Buyers

Chiavi in mano contracts consolidate land plot, design, construction, and finishes under one coordinator. Milestone payments typically align with foundation completion, structural closure, MEP installation, and final habitability rather than fragmented invoices to masons and electricians.

Independent legal review remains mandatory. Buyers should verify builder solvency, bank guarantees on deposits, penalty clauses for delay, and exact inclusion lists for kitchens, bathrooms, and outdoor terraces before compromesso signatures.

The [buy property Italy foreigner](/guides/buy-property-italy-foreigner/) guide explains codice fiscale, rogito steps, and reciprocity rules. EU citizens face no purchase restrictions. Non-EU buyers from reciprocity countries follow the same rogito path with notary and tax registration.

Agricultural land conversions and rural building permits differ by contrada. Technical due diligence on land category, buildable area, and septic versus municipal sewage capacity prevents post-handover disputes common in Puglia countryside sales.

## Due Diligence Checklist

Italian property risk clusters around cadastral mismatches, unauthorized layout changes, pending condominium extraordinary works, and STR licensing gaps that agents omit from English summaries. Independent avvocato and geometra review before compromesso beats post-deposit discovery of conformità blocks, CIN delisting risk, or spese spikes within the first ownership year.


1. Verify land category and buildable envelope with geometra.
2. Confirm SCIA or permit status and expected habitability timeline.
3. Review milestone payment schedule and bank guarantees on deposits.
4. Audit inclusion list: kitchen, bathrooms, landscaping, pool if marketed.
5. Check sewage: municipal connection versus compliant septic SCIA.
6. Compare POA against fixed [Ostuni Domus](/developers/ostuni-domus/) and [Carovigno new build](/projects/carovigno-villa-new-build/) alternatives.
7. Commission independent legal review of builder contract penalty clauses.
8. Model IMU, STR licensing, and cedolare secca on intended use case.
