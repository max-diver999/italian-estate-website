## Construction and Off-Plan Clarifications

E&V branding on new-build marketing does not automatically imply E&V construction guarantees unless contracts name a specific developer entity with performance bonds and bank escrow structures reviewed by avvocato. Many shops broker third-party developer inventory on Lake Como and Sardinia where buyers must underwrite builder track record independently.

Renovation projects marketed through E&V Liguria or Tuscany shops still require geometra conformità on terrace, pool, and septic claims because agency photography cannot substitute for cadastral and municipal filings. Off-plan deposits on branded marketing should never bypass Italian escrow norms even when shop concierge teams facilitate wire introductions.
