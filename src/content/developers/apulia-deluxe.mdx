## Heritage Expertise and Regulatory Navigation

Puglia trulli restoration triggers heritage oversight unavailable in standard villa construction. Apulia Deluxe manages archaeological surveys, chiancarelle roof tile sourcing, lime mortar specifications, and Soprintendenza inspection schedules that foreign buyers cannot realistically coordinate alone.

Listed heritage status, when granted, restricts future nearby development and can unlock tax benefits on restoration costs under applicable Italian cultural property incentives. Buyers should verify exact listing category and ongoing obligations with independent legal counsel despite developer permit packages.

The [heritage restricted property Italy](/guides/heritage-restricted-property-italy/) guide explains how conservation rules affect modifications, rentals, and resale. Apulia Deluxe projects typically arrive with approved plans, but post-handover changes still require heritage consultation.

Restoration techniques follow UNESCO-recognized dry-stone methods unique to Puglia. Apulia Deluxe employs local maestri trullari rather than generic contractors, reducing long-term maintenance failures common in poorly restored tourist rentals.

## Rental and Revenue Strategy

Apulia Deluxe properties suit luxury short-stay operators who can invest in professional photography, concierge services, and experience packages rather than passive long-term leases alone.

Heritage trulli near Ostuni achieve €150-250 nightly in peak season when presented with authentic story and premium amenities. The combined trulli-villa estate can target €200,000+ gross annual revenue under 60-70% occupancy at blended rates, though net returns fall after management, cleaning, platform fees, and 21-26% cedolare secca taxation.

Dual-structure estates allow segmented rentals: trulli for romantic couples, modern villa for larger groups, maximizing calendar utilization across guest profiles.

Operators must secure CIN registration and comply with Puglia short-stay rules including guest registration systems. Apulia Deluxe often introduces management partners familiar with Ostuni luxury segment compliance.
