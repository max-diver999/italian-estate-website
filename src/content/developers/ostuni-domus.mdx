## Closing verification checklist

Before compromesso on Ostuni Domus turnkey stock, verify inclusion list, handover timeline, and warranty transfer in writing.

**€470,000 inclusion audit.** Confirm pool, landscaping, parking, kitchen appliances, and air conditioning in the sales bundle. Turnkey promoters sometimes exclude gates, outdoor lighting, or pool equipment from base quotes.

**Handover timing.** Q3 2026 targets require habitability certificate readiness and snagging resolution before rogito balance. Near-completion buyers should inspect finish quality versus showroom renders physically.

**Warranty and defects.** Request developer warranty terms on structural envelope, MEP, and pool installation. Document snagging items with photos before final payment tranche.

**Condominium and STR.** Obtain draft regolamento if any shared road or gate costs apply. Verify CIN registration path for pool villa STR before underwriting summer revenue projections.

**Tax setup.** Model registration tax at 2% prima casa or 9% second home and annual IMU near €1,200-1,500 on €470,000 countryside villas. Budget 8-10% total buyer costs including notary.

**Pool compliance.** Verify pool registration with local health authorities where required and confirm filtration equipment specifications match Brindisi province standards for new private pools on countryside plots.

**Furnishing timeline.** If targeting summer 2026 STR launch, align rogito date with furnishing lead times.
