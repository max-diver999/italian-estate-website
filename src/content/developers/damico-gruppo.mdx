## Rental Strategy

Large villas with pools near Ostuni often achieve €120-180 nightly in July-August with 60-75% peak occupancy under professional management. Multi-bedroom layouts suit family STR segments that book week-long stays at higher total revenue than city apartments.

Long-term winter leases run lower (€800-1,500 monthly) but reduce management intensity. Blended gross yields of 5-7% appear on €500k-800k tickets when STR season performs; net yields fall after management, cleaning, and cedolare secca at 21% or 26% on gross rent.

## Closing verification checklist

Before compromesso on D'Amico Gruppo listings, verify seller role, land scale compliance, and construction progress in writing.

**Principal vs agent.** D'Amico may market as developer coordinator or licensed agent. Title holder on visura catastale must match compromesso counterparty. Agency mandates require explicit commission disclosure.

**Large-plot compliance.** Rascina-scale 14,788 sqm parcels need pool setback verification, access road maintenance obligations, and agricultural land class review. Agriturismo ambitions require separate municipal planning paths.

**Construction milestones.** Under-build stock needs photographed progress, permit validity dates, and contractor identity before milestone payments. Snagging lists on partial completion reduce rogito disputes.

**Rural utilities.** Confirm mains water, sewage, and electricity capacity for 300 sqm built volume. Autoclave and septic designs must meet habitability standards at final inspection.

**Tax and rental.** Model IMU on large rural plots and STR cedolare secca at 21% or 26% on gross rent. Wire purchase funds only to notaio conto provvisorio after avvocato review.
