## Foreign Buyer Off-Plan Process

EU citizens purchase freely with standard Italian off-plan compromesso, milestone payments, and rogito at handover. Non-EU reciprocity-country buyers need codice fiscale, Italian bank account, and notary-reviewed contracts with independent avvocato verifying developer milestone schedules and deposit protection structures.

Typical off-plan timeline runs 18-24 months from reservation to rogito on Nunziare VII schedules, requiring buyers to model carrying costs without rental income until handover unless assignment rights exist in contract.

Transaction stack on €400,000 off-plan purchase runs 9-11% including registration tax rates selected with commercialista for primary or second-home use before compromesso signing.
