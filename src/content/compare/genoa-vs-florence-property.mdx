## Why Investors Compare Genoa and Florence

Both cities anchor northern Italian urban investment narratives for foreign buyers who want established Italian governance without sole Milan concentration. Genoa delivers Liguria coast gateway access and superior rental yield math at roughly half Florence per sqm. Florence delivers global art-tourism branding that supports trophy resale even when net yields compress in centro palazzi.

Choosing between them is tenant and exit strategy alignment: port-sector professionals and yield landlords fit Genoa Albaro and centro stock; Anglo-American lifestyle buyers and STR operators with Italian license expertise fit Florence Oltrarno and periphery when UNESCO rules allow revenue plans.

## Price and Entry Tickets

Genoa Ponente entroterra trades near €924 per sqm offering deep value renovation plays while Albaro sea-view stock reaches €3,403 per sqm on Immobiliare.it April 2026 data. Entry two-bedroom coastal tickets often span €220,000-400,000 on elevator condominiums with parking.

Florence secondary districts list €300,000-500,000 for 70-90 sqm apartments needing selective renovation while centro UNESCO tickets exceed €600,000-1,000,000 for comparable floor area without secure parking.

## Rental Strategies

Genoa Q1 2026 rent growth leadership at plus 6.4% supports landlord pricing on furnished centro and Albaro leases when parking and elevator compliance are documented. A €300,000 Albaro bilocale at €1,150 monthly yields about 4.6% gross before IMU and cedolare secca.

Florence licensed STR on Oltrarno apartments can exceed 4% gross in peak seasons but faces UNESCO enforcement and neighbor scrutiny compressing net returns. Long-term academic leases deliver 3-3.5% gross with more stable occupancy than centro STR models blocked by new license bans.

## Connectivity and Employment

Genoa Cristoforo Colombo Airport and Liguria coastal rail position the city as Riviera gateway toward [Santa Margherita Ligure](/areas/santa-margherita-ligure/) and [Portofino](/areas/portofino/) eastward and [Savona](/areas/savona/) westward within 30-60 minutes.

Florence relies on tourism, hospitality, and creative industries with AV rail to Rome and Bologna supporting consultant tenants but without Genoa port-economy depth on industrial salary leases.

## Regulation and Renovation Risk

Florence UNESCO buffer imposes soprintendenza review on façade changes extending renovation timelines on centro palazzi purchased from photographs. New STR license bans in UNESCO centro block scalable Airbnb models that agents still market informally on legacy listings.

Genoa hillside and centro stock requires geotechnical diligence on steep slopes while Albaro elevator condominiums concentrate risk on condominium spese and energy certificates rather than heritage capex uncertainty alone.
