## Why Investors Compare Turin and Milan

Both cities anchor northwest Italy urban investment for foreign buyers seeking northern governance without sole Milan concentration risk. Turin delivers Politecnico student pipelines, automotive and design employment, and per sqm tickets often half Milan averages with superior gross yield math on furnished long leases. Milan delivers global corporate tenant depth, AV European hub connectivity, and resale liquidity that supports faster exits when priced for international buyers.

Choosing between them aligns tenant profile and exit strategy: university and yield landlords fit Turin San Salvario and Crocetta; corporate expat and trophy resale buyers fit Milan Navigli and northwest new-build even when net yields compress on higher tickets.

## Rental Strategies
