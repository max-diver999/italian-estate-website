## Why Savona Belongs on a Liguria Coast Shortlist

Savona offers Liguria coast exposure at tickets often 25-40% below [Genoa](/areas/genoa/) Albaro premiums and 30-50% below [Sanremo](/areas/sanremo/) corso sea-view stock while retaining port-economy employment and regional rail connectivity. Value-focused investors use Savona as Ponente anchor before upgrading east toward Santa Margherita or west toward French border markets.

Priamar fortress hillside and centro palazzi provide character stock distinct from anonymous suburban condominiums, while Albissola Marina ceramic villages add lifestyle branding minutes from Savona services.

## District Guide

### Centro and Priamar fortress

Historic centro lanes and Priamar hillside command €2,600-3,200 per sqm on restored apartments with partial sea views toward the harbour. UNESCO and portico character suits lifestyle owners accepting renovation compliance and limited parking.

### Savona beach and Zinola promenade

Coastal condominiums from 1960s-1990s deliver elevator stock with parking and promenade walkability at €2,200-2,800 per sqm. Default zones for port-sector furnished leases and Genoa commuters seeking coast access at value tickets.

### Albissola Marina and Albisola Superiore

Ceramic-art village branding attracts Italian lifestyle buyers within 10 minutes of Savona. Sea-view stock trades toward €3,000-3,800 per sqm on portal snapshots with stronger domestic resale than Savona periphery alone.

### Ponente periphery value

Inland comuni toward Piedmont border offer €1,500-2,200 per sqm renovation plays with car dependency and thinner foreign liquidity.

## Rental Market

Port-sector workers, cruise contractors, and Genoa commuters form stable long-term tenant pools on furnished twelve-month contracts. A €240,000 promenade bilocale at €1,000 monthly yields about 4.2% gross before Italian taxes.

STR on licensed seafront units supplements income during summer beach season though occupancy models should remain conservative relative to Sanremo festival-driven peaks.

## Industrial Port Character

Savona port activity creates employment supporting rental demand but also industrial noise and visual exposure on select central addresses. Visit properties during weekday operations before compromesso and disclose port proximity honestly in tenant marketing to avoid lease disputes.

Buyers comparing Savona against Tuscan coast value in [Versilia](/areas/versilia/) should weigh Liguria geotechnical risk and port character against Tuscan beach branding and different STR regulatory density.
