## Why Santa Margherita Belongs on a Liguria Coast Shortlist

Santa Margherita Ligure bridges Genoa urban scale and Portofino global trophy scarcity, offering walkable marina lifestyle, year-round restaurants and clinics, and apartment inventory depth rare in Portofino village itself. Foreign buyers treat Santa Margherita as the practical Riviera address when Portofino listings show single-digit active stock at €1,000,000+ entry.

Yacht tourism from Portofino spillover, ferry connections, and regional rail to Genoa support both STR and long-term furnished lease strategies on sea-view terraces with parking when documented in lease annexes.

## District Guide

### Harbour promenade and marina front

Harbour-facing apartments command €6,500-10,000 per sqm on terraces with yacht basin sightlines. STR operators target April-October peaks with licensed inventory and professional management handling marina-event weekends.

### Sea-view terraces and Corte lanes

Elevated sea-view stock trades €5,000-7,500 per sqm with parking premiums separating quick resale from stale hillside listings without garage solutions. British and Swiss buyers dominate furnished long-lease demand on twelve-month contracts.

### Hillside character zones

Hillside lanes offer character apartments at €4,000-5,500 per sqm with geotechnical diligence requirements and landscape vincolo on terrace expansions. Visit properties during rain season to assess drainage before capex budgets lock.

### Rapallo border spillover

Eastern border zones toward Rapallo deliver value at €3,500-4,800 per sqm with five-minute rail access to Santa Margherita services, suited to yield investors accepting slightly reduced prestige branding.
