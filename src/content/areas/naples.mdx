## Why Naples Matters for Property Investors

Naples is Italy's third-largest city economy and the operational hub for Campania tourism, port logistics, and southern Italy professional services. Foreign buyers historically overweighted Rome and Milan while Naples offered deeper yield on hillsides with Gulf views and faster access to Amalfi than Rome ever provides.

The investment case splits into three lanes: yield on Vomero and Chiaia residential, STR on centro storico and waterfront-adjacent stock, and value-add on UNESCO palazzi requiring renovation discipline. None of these lanes behaves like Milan off-plan towers; underwriting must respect seismic codes, conformità culture, and neighbourhood reputation that still moves faster than crime statistics justify.

For capital-city comparison, read [Naples vs Rome property investment](/compare/naples-vs-rome-property-investment/) before choosing ticket size.

## District Architecture: Where Money Flows

Short-term rental income requires valid CIN registration, commune SCIA or SUAR paths where applicable, Alloggiati Web guest filing, and tourist tax collection remitted to comune. First-property STR income may use 21% cedolare secca; a second property triggers 26% — model net after platform fees, cleaning, and void months not peak-event screenshots alone.

### Vomero: the default foreign hill

Vomero sits above centro with funicular and metro links, tree-lined streets, and views toward Vesuvius and the bay. Stock is predominantly 1960s-1990s condominiums with elevators, a critical filter for tenants and resale.

Investment angle: balanced yield and appreciation with lower operational drama than centro. Two-bedroom apartments from €350k-550k often rent furnished to professionals and medical sector workers tied to city hospitals.

### Chiaia: prestige waterfront adjacency

Chiaia combines shopping streets, seafront passeggiata culture, and addresses that signal status to Italian buyers from northern cities. Prices per sqm exceed Vomero on prime streets.

Investment angle: capital preservation with moderate yield compression. Foreign buyers from UK and US appear on lifestyle purchases with optional STR in summer.

### Posillipo and Riviera di Chiaia

Posillipo delivers cliff villas and exclusive apartments with direct sea orientation. Liquidity is thinner but trophy resale can surprise on the right Gulf-view terrace.

### Centro storico UNESCO

Spacannapoli density, palazzi, and archaeological sensitivity create renovation complexity. Soprintendenza oversight on facades is routine. STR works when licensed but building rules vary street by street.

## Rental Market: Long-Term, STR, and Hybrid

Naples rental economics favour investors who blend academic year tenants with summer tourism overflow.

| Strategy | Gross yield band | Notes |

Winter occupancy on STR without long-term anchor can collapse below 40% unless you price for business travel and cruise season shoulder weeks.

## Foreign buyer execution playbook

Foreign buyers succeed in Naples when they localise management and legal review rather than copying Rome playbooks. Appoint an avvocato in Campania before viewing trips so compromesso drafts arrive with seismic and conformità clauses tailored to your target district. Remote offers without geometric survey are high risk on centro palazzi where room counts on visura rarely match terraces enclosed in the 1990s.

Mortgage brokers with Naples bank relationships can finance Vomero elevators stock faster than centro UNESCO units with open abusi files. Budget 60-90 days from accepted offer to rogito when conformità is clean; centro renovations under Soprintendenza oversight can stretch timelines beyond six months before you can legally rent STR.

Tenant sourcing splits between long-term furnished portals for professionals, university contracts for hillside micro-units, and licensed STR platforms for centro tourism. Mixing strategies without regolamento permission invites condominium fines. Hire operators who document CIN registration and guest police reporting before marketing begins.

Insurance packages for furnished landlords differ from owner-occupier policies; confirm coverage includes tenant liability and water damage in older buildings. IMU bills arrive annually; non-resident owners need local commercialista for cedolare secca election before first rent invoice.

Resale preparation requires conformità dossiers and elevator certificates buyers expect in Vomero. Chiaia trophy units resell to Italian Milan-Rome families when priced in euros per sqm with parking deed, not when marketed only on Gulf view photos.

If yield is primary, underwrite Vomero at 4.5% gross before costs and stress-test 15% void months. If lifestyle and Amalfi gateway matter, Chiaia may justify 3.5% gross with lower voids. Centro STR can exceed 5% gross in July but needs 40% winter discounting unless paired with academic tenants.

## Closing verification checklist

Use this Italy buyer checklist before compromesso signature on Naples residential stock. Your independent avvocato should verify every line; the selling agent does not represent you at rogito.

**Identity and tax setup.** Confirm codice fiscale matches passport spelling. Non-EU buyers need MAECI reciprocity confirmation before deposit release. Open Italian bank account or confirm wire capability for euro sums. Discuss prima casa election with tax counsel if residency within 18 months is realistic.

**Title and cadastral consistency.** Request visura catastale storica and compare room counts against on-site survey. Seaside and hill apartments often show terrace encroachments; verify abusi file before rogito. Seismic classification certificates matter for pre-1970 Vomero and centro palazzi.

**Condominium and STR.** Obtain regolamento and confirm affitti brevi permitted. CIN registration with Comune di Napoli requires compliant floor plans and guest reporting workflows. Buildings near Spaccanapoli may ban sub-30-day stays entirely.

**Coastal and parking.** Confirm deeded parking or long-term lease in writing. Check coastal planning restrictions on ground-floor units. Review flood and storm-surge risk maps for Chiaia waterfront addresses.

**Closing economics.** Budget registration tax at 9% on second homes (2% only with prima casa and residency registration within 18 months). Add notary 1-2%, survey, and agency fees. Wire only to notaio conto provvisorio.

**Seismic retrofit quotes.** Centro and Vomero pre-1970 stock may need structural upgrades to rent legally to corporate tenants. Obtain engineer quotes before compromesso if seller claims retrofit completed.

**Noise and STR saturation.** Walk the block at 22:00 on Friday before buying centro STR units. Nightlife corridors can trigger condominium votes to ban tourist stays after your purchase.

**Insurance and liability.** Confirm building insurance covers common areas and that your landlord policy extends to furnished tenant equipment if leasing STR.
