## Why Sanremo Belongs on a Liguria Coast Shortlist

Sanremo anchors western Liguria between French Riviera pricing gravity and Genoa urban scale, offering foreign buyers the deepest mid-tier Riviera inventory outside Genoa city itself. Milan weekend owners, French cross-border purchasers, and German retirees dominate demand for sea-view apartments with terraces above the harbour and corso Italia services.

Unlike [Portofino](/areas/portofino/) scarcity at €8,000-15,000+ per sqm, Sanremo delivers walkable coastal lifestyle from about €400,000 on [sea view apartment](/projects/sanremo-sea-view-apartments/) stock with STR potential during Song Festival and spring flower-market calendars.

## District Guide

District selection drives both yield and resale liquidity: centro premiums carry UNESCO or tourism branding while peripheral corridors deliver higher gross yields on lower per sqm tickets with car dependency tradeoffs. Verify parking deeds, elevator conformity, and STR regolamento before marketing furnished leases to hospital, university, or corporate tenants.

### Corso Italia and Porto Sole

Main commercial and seafront corridors command €4,500-6,500 per sqm on restored apartments with partial to full sea views. STR operators target festival weeks and summer peaks when CIN and building regolamento permit tourist sublets.

### Poggio and elevated lanes

Hillside terraces above Aurelia trade premium view lines with traffic noise trade-offs on lower floors. Top-floor units with parking dominate Milan owner demand for weekend residences with optional licensed STR.

### Bussana Vecchia and artisan hills

Bussana Vecchia attracts lifestyle buyers accepting village character and limited parking at lower per sqm than corso stock. Resale liquidity trails corso sea-view inventory for foreign exit strategies.

### West Liguria value hills

Inland comuni toward Toscana border offer €2,800-3,500 per sqm value plays with car dependency and geotechnical diligence requirements on steep slopes.
