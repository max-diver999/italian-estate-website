## District Guide: Centro, Lungomare, and Hillside

### Centro Storico and Lungomare

Character: Restaurants, gelato corridors, beach access stairs, summer nightlife, family tourism.

Price Range: €1,000-1,400 per m² centro; lungomare €1,200-1,800 per sqm with views.

Rental Profile: STR dominates July-August; shoulder weeks expand with Italian family bookings in June and September.

Yield Potential: 6-8% gross on licensed two-bedroom stock when management and dynamic pricing are professional.

Challenges: Parking August congestion, noise near beach bars, salt air maintenance.

### Talao and Hillside Contrade

Character: Panoramic views, quieter nights, pool villas, longer drives to beach.

Price Range: €900-1,300 per m² built; villas €300,000-500,000.

Rental Profile: Weekly family STR and winter remote-worker LT mixes.

Yield Potential: 5-7% gross depending on pool compliance and access road quality.
